What is Web WhatsApp?

Web WhatsApp is an extension of the WhatsApp mobile app, enabling users to access their messages and contacts from a computer's web browser. It mirrors conversations and messages from the mobile app to the desktop, making it convenient for users who work on their computers all day.

WhatsApp Web allows users to send messages, images, videos, and documents from a larger screen with the convenience of a full keyboard.

Key Features of WhatsApp Web

QR Code Login

Scan a QR code with your phone to connect instantly—no installation required.

Instant Messaging

Send and receive messages from your computer with real-time synchronization.

File & Media Sharing

Easily send documents, images, and videos with a simple drag-and-drop.

End-to-End Encryption

Your messages remain private with WhatsApp’s secure encryption technology.

Desktop Notifications

Stay updated with real-time message alerts even while working.

Multi-Device Support

Use WhatsApp on multiple devices without keeping your phone online.

How to Set Up Web WhatsApp?

1

On your computer, open a web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge) and go to web.whatsapp.com.

2

On your smartphone, open WhatsApp. Go to “Settings” (iOS) or “Menu” (Android) and select “Linked devices.”

3

Tap “Link a device” in your WhatsApp mobile app and scan the QR code displayed on the WhatsApp Web screen.

4

Once the QR code is scanned, your conversations will appear on the web interface. You’re now ready to start messaging on WhatsApp Web.

How to Use Web WhatsApp?

  • Step 1: Open web.whatsapp.com on your desktop browser.
  • Step 2: Make sure you have the WhatsApp app installed on your phone (Android or iOS)..
  • Step 3: On Android mobile, open WhatsApp, tap the 3-dots in the top-right corner and select the 'Settings' option.
  • Step 4: On iPhone mobile, go to Settings and Linked devices.
  • Step 5: Tap 'Link Device' and scan the QR code displayed on your computer screen.
  • Step 6: After scanning the connected QR code, your chats will appear on WhatsApp Web, allowing you to send messages, access media, and more. Your phone must remain connected to the internet for the session to remain active.
